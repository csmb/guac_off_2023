The 5th Annual Indian Summer Guac-Off is a party that showcases the best guacamoles that The City has to offer.

The Rules

  1. Your guacamole must use at least 7 avocados.
  2. If you don't bring a guacamole, you're encouraged to bring beer.
  3. No matter what you decide to bring, bring your friends!
  4. We'll provide the chips, mystery prizes, and the coveted Guacamole Glory Trophy.

The Judging

You decide who has the best guacamole in The City. The winner to be crowned with the Guacamole Glory Trophy, bragging rights until next Indian summer, the title of official Guacamole of San Francisco, and a secret mystery prize!
