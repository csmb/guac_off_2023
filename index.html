The 12th Annual San Francisco
Guac Off
Eat guacamole with your friends and neighbors
Saturday, Sept. 9, 2023th
1pm 'til the last chip is chewed

The 12th Annual San Francisco Guac Off is The Party that showcases the best guacamole The City has to offer.

The Rules

  1. Your guacamole must use at least 8 avocados.
  2. Bring guacamole. Or your friend. Or a beverage.
  3. No matter what you decide to bring, bring your friends!
  4. We'll provide the chips, mystery prizes, and the coveted Guacamole Glory Trophy.

The Judging

Everyone votes for their favorite. Celebrity judges give out select awards. The winner receives The Guacamole Glory Trophy and bragging rights until summer, the title of official Guacamole of San Francisco, and a secret mystery prize!

The Prizes

The top voted chef receives The Guacamole Glory Trophy and bragging rights until summer. The title of official Guacamole of San Francisco, a secret mystery prize! And maybe you even end up on KRON 4.

The Location

The Farmhouse Mansion
